Why Add Your Medical History?

Adding your medical history helps in keeping track of your past diagnoses, treatments, and other relevant medical information. It ensures that healthcare providers have accurate and comprehensive information to provide the best possible care. By maintaining an up-to-date medical history, you can also manage your health more effectively and make informed decisions about your treatment.

Why Add Your Medications?

Keeping a record of your medications helps in managing your treatment effectively. It allows healthcare providers to understand your current regimen and make necessary adjustments to optimize your care. Accurate medication history prevents drug interactions and ensures you receive the best possible outcomes.
